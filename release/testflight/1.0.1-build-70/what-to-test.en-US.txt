This build focuses on faster, but still stable, automatic RDS announcements for screen reader users.

Please pay special attention to:
- RDS: on strong stations, automatic reading should react faster than before, but still avoid partially decoded or unfinished text.
- RDS: station name, radio text, and PI should be announced after a short stabilization period without feeling delayed.
- RDS: full reading mode should avoid truncated fragments while the incoming data is still changing quickly.
- General playback and accessibility stability after the update.
