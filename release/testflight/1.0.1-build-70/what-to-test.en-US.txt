This build focuses on more stable automatic RDS announcements for screen reader users.

Please pay special attention to:
- RDS: on strong stations, automatic reading should no longer speak partially decoded or unfinished text.
- RDS: station name, radio text, and PI should only be announced after the content has stabilized.
- RDS: full reading mode should avoid truncated fragments while the incoming data is still changing quickly.
- General playback and accessibility stability after the update.
