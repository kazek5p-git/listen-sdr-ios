The main changes in this build are the new typed-frequency behavior and fixes around settings backup and accessibility.

Please focus on:
- Tuning: the new "Typed frequency" setting lets you choose automatic tuning or manual confirmation.
- Tuning: in automatic mode, a valid typed frequency should tune after a short pause without the Apply button.
- Tuning: in manual mode, the Apply button should return and tuning should not start on its own.
- Settings: exporting a settings backup should let you choose the save location, and restoring should let you pick a backup file.
- Accessibility: Magic Tap should work even when playback is allowed together with other apps.
