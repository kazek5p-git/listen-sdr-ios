This build focuses on accessibility refinements and a smoother first-run experience.

Please pay special attention to:
- Tutorial: showing on first launch, turning automatic presentation on or off, and reopening it later from Settings.
- VoiceOver: fast rotor navigation through receiver bookmarks and presets without opening extra screens.
- Volume slider: correct localized percentage reading in different languages, without a hard-coded English “percent”.
- Bookmarks and presets: spoken selection confirmations after tuning from bookmark, band, and preset lists.
- Squelch: enabling squelch should no longer retune the receiver to FM and should still behave correctly after reconnect.
