This build expands diagnostics logging on iPhone.

- the app now records transitions between active, inactive, and background
- entering background and returning to foreground are logged
- backend runtime policy changes are logged
- AVAudioSession interruption and route-change events are logged
- FM-DX now records richer audio context around screen lock, audio route changes, and resume
