Listen SDR Support

If you need help with Listen SDR, want to report a bug, or want to suggest an improvement, use one of the options below.

Support requests are most useful when they clearly describe the receiver type, the device, the iOS version, and whether the issue affects accessibility, recording, reconnect behavior, or audio routing.

Contact

  • Email: kazek5p@gmail.com
  • GitHub issues: listen-sdr-ios/issues

What helps when reporting a problem

  • Receiver type: FM-DX Webserver, KiwiSDR, or OpenWebRX
  • Device and iOS version
  • Short description of what you expected and what happened instead
  • Whether the issue happens after reconnect, during recording, or with accessibility features enabled
  • Whether you were using VoiceOver, AirPlay, or an external audio route

Accessibility reports are welcome

Listen SDR is intentionally developed with accessibility in mind. Reports about VoiceOver behavior, focus order, spoken feedback, grouped controls, and everyday usability for blind and low-vision listeners are especially valuable.

Feature suggestions

If you want to propose an improvement, the most helpful reports explain the listening workflow you are trying to achieve, the backend involved, and what would make the task faster or more accessible in practice.
